Vission

To become a well-loved local delicacy that transforms cassava into a symbol of creativity, resilience, and sweetness--showchasing that even bitterness can be turned into something delightful and meaningful.

Mission

Cassavayaki is committed to producing high-quality, affordable, and delicious cassava-based pastries by using locally sourced ingredients, innovative flavors, and careful preparation. We aim to provide customers with a unique taste experience while supporting local farmers and promoting the value of cassava as a nutritious and versatile food crop.
